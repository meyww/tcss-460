This application allows end users to calculate various health-related metrics, including Body Mass Index (BMI), Body Fat, Ideal Weight, and Calories Burned.
This website calculator provides the following functionalities:
_ Body Mass Index (BMI) Calculation: Calculate your BMI based on your weight and height. BMI is a useful indicator of whether you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.
_ Body Fat Calculation: Estimate your body fat percentage using different methods.This helps to assess your overall body composition.
_ Ideal Weight Calculation: Determine your ideal weight based on your gender, height, and body frame. This can be a helpful reference for setting weight goals.
_ Calories Burned Calculation: Find out the approximate number of calories burned during various activities and exercises. This is planning your daily calorie expenditure.
